ABOUT US

SMU students are required to do everything manually,From submitting their applications ,receiving their academic transcripts
accessing their timetables.

This website is created to alleviate the burden on SMU students and to make their lives easier

ON THIS WEBSITE,STUDENTS CAN:


-Receive assignments.
-Receive previous past paper questions for tests and exam preparation.
-Check their academic transcripts.
-Watch videos uploaded by lectures in their own time at their own pace.

SMU was established when the Minister of Higher Education and Training, Dr Blade Nzimande in terms of section 20 of the
Higher Education Act 1997 (Act 101 of 1997) promulgated the Sefako Makgatho Health Sciences University (SMU) in the
SA Government Gazette no: 37658 of 16 May 2014.
